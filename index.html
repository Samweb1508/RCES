RAJARSHI COLLEGE OF EDUCATION & SKILL

Rajarshi College of Education & Skill is an initiative of Rajarshi Welfare Trust. The Trust was founded on 2017 by eminent philanthropic personalities of Tripura to help the people in need. As an initiative, the trustees decided to lay the foundation of Rajarshi College of Education & Skill. The college started its maiden journey admist the Covid pandemic in 2020 with merely 8 students and a few highly qualified Professors.

Rajarshi College of Education & Skill offers General and Honours Bachelor degree in selective Arts and Science streams.

RCES offers General & Honours Arts courses in the following streams:

    ENGLISH
    HISTORY
    POLITICAL SCIENCE
    ENGLISH
    EDUCATION

In science streams, RCES offers General & Honours in the following subjects:

    MATHEMATICS
    PHYSICS
    CHEMISTRY

Concerned about the employability of the students, RCES offers Govt. approved Skill Courses in the following:

    BEAUTY & WELLNESS
    BANKING, FINANCE & INSURANCE
    RUBBER
    MEDIA & ENTERTAINMENT
    RETAIL
